Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

Your privacy is important to us at BrokeBackDoc. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit brokebackdoc.com or book an advisory session.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us. This includes:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, and phone number when you reach out via email or WhatsApp.
  • Booking Information: When you schedule a session, our third-party booking provider (Calendly) collects the necessary information to secure your appointment.

2. Affiliate Tracking and Cookies

This website uses affiliate links. When you click on an affiliate link (such as an Amazon link), a tracking cookie is placed in your browser to attribute any resulting purchase back to BrokeBackDoc. These third-party platforms (like Amazon or Pinterest) have their own privacy policies governing how they collect and use this data.

3. How We Use Your Information

Any information you provide is used strictly to communicate with you, manage your bookings, and provide the mentorship sessions requested. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to outside parties.

4. Third-Party Services

We use third-party tools to operate this site effectively:

  • Calendly: For scheduling sessions.
  • WhatsApp: For direct communication (if initiated by you).
Please refer to the respective privacy policies of these services to understand how they handle your data.
