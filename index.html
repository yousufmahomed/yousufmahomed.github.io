If you are exhausted by the daily, invisible marathon of chronic back issues, I am here to listen. The medical system treats the physical body, but it rarely helps you understand the complex jargon, the mental toll, or how to practically rebuild your life.

My Journey

For ten years, I served as an Emergency Physician and Head of Department. I had moderate back issues for about a year, but I pushed through. Then, one day on the job while seeing a patient, multiple discs herniated. I was left physically bent over, unable to stand straight until my first procedure in 2022.

I have been through seven procedures since then. The most recent surgery was to remove massive amounts of scar tissue compressing my nerve root. Constant, severe neuralgia isn't just physical pain; it is deeply tied to the dark places pain takes you. It strips away the person you used to be.

I built this space to offer you a chance to discuss your feelings and your own journey. I can help translate the medical jargon so you actually understand your condition, but mostly, I am here to listen.
