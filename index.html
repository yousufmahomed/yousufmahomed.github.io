My Journey

For ten years, I served as an Emergency Physician and Head of Department. I had moderate back issues for about a year, but I pushed through. Then, one day on the job while seeing a patient, multiple discs herniated. I was left physically bent over, unable to stand straight until my first procedure in 2022.

I have been through seven procedures since then. The most recent surgery was to remove massive amounts of scar tissue compressing my nerve root. I have suffered physically, but I know firsthand that the true weight of this pain cannot be understood by anyone who hasn't lived it.

I use AI avatars now to help me connect more deeply with you. It allows me to maintain the high level of professionalism I held before my injury, and honestly, creating this space is therapeutic for me. I am still on my own journey, and I accept that it may never fully end.

I built this space to offer you a chance to discuss your feelings and your own journey. I can help translate the medical jargon so you actually understand your condition, but mostly, I am here to listen.
