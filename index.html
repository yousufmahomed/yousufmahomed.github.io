My Journey

For ten years, I served as an Emergency Physician and Head of Department. I had moderate back issues for about a year, but I pushed through. Then, one day on the job while seeing a patient, multiple discs herniated. I was left physically bent over, unable to stand straight until my first procedure in 2022.

I have been through seven procedures since then. The most recent surgery was to remove massive amounts of scar tissue compressing my nerve root. I have suffered physically, but I know firsthand that the true weight of this pain cannot be understood by anyone who hasn't lived it. Constant, severe neuralgia isn't just physical pain; it is deeply tied to severe depression and dark places. It strips away the person you used to be.

I use AI avatars now to help me connect more deeply with you. It allows me to maintain the high level of professionalism I held before my injury, and honestly, creating this space is therapeutic for me. I am still on my own journey, and I accept that it may never fully end.

I built this space to offer you a chance to discuss your feelings and your own journey. I can help translate the medical jargon so you actually understand your condition, but mostly, I am here to listen. While my personal battle is rooted in the spine, any sufferer of chronic pain is welcome here.

Professional Disclaimer & Scope of Service

The BrokeBackDoc Advisory & Mentorship sessions are designed to provide peer support, health system navigation, and mental resilience coaching based on lived experience and general medical knowledge.

Please Note:

  1. No Doctor-Patient Relationship: These sessions do not constitute a formal clinical doctor-patient relationship.
  2. Not Clinical Advice: I do not provide clinical diagnoses, medical prescriptions, or surgical recommendations during these sessions.
  3. Primary Care: All clinical decisions must be discussed with your primary treating physician or surgeon. This service is a supplement to—not a replacement for—professional medical care.
  4. Emergencies: If you are experiencing a medical emergency (such as sudden loss of bowel/bladder control or acute paralysis), please contact your local emergency services immediately.

By booking a session, you acknowledge that you understand the advisory nature of this mentorship.
